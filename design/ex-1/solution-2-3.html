What is Typography?

Typography is the art and technique of arranging type to make written language legible, readable, and visually appealing. It involves choosing fonts, sizes, spacing, and line heights that help in structuring content for better readability.

Why Typography Matters?

The right combination of fonts and text elements enhances readability and improves user experience. Proper use of headings and body text provides structure, making it easier for users to scan and navigate content.

Using Custom Fonts in Web Design

Custom fonts like Geist give your website a unique personality, allowing you to establish a cohesive brand identity. Fonts contribute to the overall tone of the content, whether it's formal, casual, or playful.
