About Indian Railways

Indian Railways is one of the world's largest rail networks, serving millions of passengers daily. We are committed to providing safe, efficient, and comfortable travel experiences across India.

  • Extensive network covering over 67,000 km
  • More than 13,000 passenger trains operating daily
  • Connecting over 7,000 stations across India
  • Eco-friendly transportation for a sustainable future
